About Us

National Designated Authority for Climate Finance

Who We Are

The National Designated Authority (NDA) of Eritrea is the official entity responsible for interfacing with the Green Climate Fund. Established under the Ministry of Land, Water and Environment, we serve as the national focal point for climate finance coordination, ensuring that climate projects align with national priorities and international standards.

Our Role

  • Coordinate climate finance activities at the national level
  • Endorse climate projects and programs for GCF funding
  • Facilitate stakeholder engagement and capacity building
  • Ensure alignment with national development priorities
  • Monitor and evaluate project implementation

The Readiness Program

The Readiness Eritrea project is supported by the Green Climate Fund's Readiness and Preparatory Support Programme. This initiative provides countries with the resources needed to strengthen institutional capacities, direct access to funding, and develop a robust pipeline of climate-ready projects.

Through this program, Eritrea is building the necessary foundation to effectively mobilize and manage climate finance, ensuring that our climate action efforts are sustainable, impactful, and aligned with global climate goals.

Our Partners

We work closely with a network of national and international partners, including government ministries, development agencies, civil society organizations, and the private sector. Together, we are driving meaningful climate action that benefits communities across Eritrea.
